Pediatric FB triage — decision support

⚠️ Before you begin

This is a testing build. HipPocketEASE is a decision-support tool based on the 2015 NASPGHAN pediatric foreign body ingestion algorithms (Kramer et al.).

This tool is not a substitute for clinical judgment, institutional protocols, or direct consultation with Pediatric Gastroenterology. It does not establish a legal standard of care. You remain fully responsible for all clinical decisions.

No patient data entered here is transmitted or stored off this device. Session history is kept locally on this device only and can be cleared at any time from the menu.

About HipPocketEASE

Version: Testing Build 0.1

A pediatric foreign body ingestion triage aid based on the 2015 NASPGHAN algorithms (Kramer et al., JPGN 60:562-574).

Built in conjunction with Dr. Julia Fritz, MaineHealth Medical Partners. Originally a Python CS 5001 final project by Patrick Cobb, Northeastern University.

Kramer RE, Lerner DG, Lin T, et al. Management of Ingested Foreign Bodies in Children: A Clinical Report of the NASPGHAN Endoscopy Committee. J Pediatr Gastroenterol Nutr. 2015;60(4):562-574.
